Menu bar popover with device priorities

Auto-Switch

Connects your highest-priority device automatically. Plug in your headphones, they're already selected.

Drag to Reorder

Set device priorities with simple drag-and-drop. Your preferences persist across restarts.

Volume Control

Adjust speaker and microphone levels directly from the menu bar. Scroll wheel supported.

Device Memory

Remembers your devices by UID. Reconnect at any port—your priorities stay intact.

Ignore List

Hide devices you never use. Keep your list clean and focused on what matters.

Lightweight

Native Swift app. No Electron, no web views. Sits quietly in your menu bar.

No special permissions required. Audio Priority uses standard macOS audio APIs to manage your devices.
